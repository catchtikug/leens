About MediSee International

Pioneering global medicine safety through innovative monitoring and collaborative healthcare solutions.

Our Mission

At MediSee International, we're committed to transforming global healthcare through comprehensive medicine safety monitoring. Our platform bridges the gap between healthcare providers, pharmaceutical companies, and regulatory bodies to create a safer medication environment worldwide.

Vision

A world where every medication prescribed is backed by real-time safety data.

Purpose

Empowering healthcare professionals with actionable insights to protect patients.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a team of pharmacologists and data scientists, MediSee began as a research project at a leading university hospital.

2015
Research Beginnings

Initial concept developed to track antibiotic resistance patterns in a single hospital network.

2017
First Deployment

Pilot program launched across 12 hospitals with promising results in early detection.

2019
Global Expansion

Platform expanded to include adverse drug reaction monitoring with international partners.

2023
Current Reach

Now serving over 200 healthcare facilities across 35 countries with real-time analytics.
